Projects & Research

RStudio Project: Weather Analysis

An interactive exploration of Lake Monona winter trends using R (tidyverse, ggplot2).

We examined first‑freeze timing and winter duration over 100+ years. The visualizations revealed a trend toward shorter winters and later freezes, suggesting local climate shifts.

Stata Project: Labor & Betting Models

Econometric analysis of mortgage approval disparities and college basketball betting markets.

Using linear probability and probit models, I found a 4.27 ppt higher approval likelihood for white applicants, and confirmed that betting spreads have strong predictive power.
