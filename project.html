Projects & Research

RStudio Project: Weather Analysis

An interactive exploration of Lake Monona winter trends using R (tidyverse, ggplot2).

We examined first‑freeze timing and winter duration over 100+ years. Visualizations revealed shorter winters and later freezes—evidence of local climate shifts.

Stata Project: Labor & Betting Models

Econometric analysis of mortgage approval disparities and college basketball betting markets.

Linear probability and probit models showed a 4.27 ppt higher approval likelihood for white applicants, and strong predictive power in betting spreads.
